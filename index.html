Who Are We?

We are Echelon

Forget coding in isolation. Echelon is CDGI's central hub for builders, breakers, and creators.

We are a student-run collective passionate about one thing: turning caffeine-fueled ideas into tangible technology. Our mission is to build a collaborative community where every student, regardless of skill level, can learn, build, and lead the next wave of tech.

Whether you've just written your first "Hello, World!" or you're a seasoned hackathon champion, you have a place here.
